Frequently asked questions

Is FamPass really free?

Yes! The free version lets you browse all events and see details for events happening today. Premium unlocks extended access, calendar sync, and removes ads.

Where do the events come from?

We aggregate events from over 30 trusted sources including Ticketmaster, Eventbrite, local libraries, museums, city recreation departments, and venue websites. Events are updated twice daily.

How do you ensure events are family-friendly?

Every event goes through a multi-layer content filter that screens out adult-only events, bar crawls, 21+ shows, and other non-family content. Our team also manually reviews flagged events.

What areas do you cover?

We currently cover Orange County and Los Angeles. This includes events from cities like Irvine, Anaheim, Pasadena, Santa Monica, and dozens more. We're expanding to additional metros soon.

Can I suggest an event?

Absolutely! The app has a "Suggest an Event" feature in the More menu. You can submit events directly, including recurring events. Our team reviews and approves submissions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > FamPass > Cancel. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
