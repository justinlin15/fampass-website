Common Questions

How do I reset my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage or cancel your FamPass subscription.

Events aren't loading. What should I do?

Make sure you have an internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, email us at dev@fampass.io with your device model and iOS version.

I found incorrect event information.

We aggregate from many sources and sometimes details change. Please let us know through the feedback form above and we'll correct it.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing dev@fampass.io. We'll remove your data within 30 days as required by Apple's guidelines.
