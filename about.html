About FamPass

Helping families explore their neighborhoods.

Our Mission

FamPass was built by a parent, for parents. Finding fun, age-appropriate activities for your kids shouldn't require checking a dozen websites, Facebook groups, and community boards. We built FamPass to bring it all together in one place.

We aggregate events from over 30 trusted sources — libraries, museums, city parks, ticketing platforms, and local venues — and update them twice daily. Every event is automatically screened for family-friendliness so you can browse with confidence.

What We Cover

FamPass currently serves families across two of Southern California's largest metro areas:

Orange County

Irvine, Anaheim, Huntington Beach, Costa Mesa, Mission Viejo, Laguna Beach, San Juan Capistrano, and dozens more cities across OC.

Los Angeles

Pasadena, Santa Monica, Glendale, Burbank, Culver City, and communities across the greater LA area.

We're actively working on expanding to additional metro areas. Stay tuned!
